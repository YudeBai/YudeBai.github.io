Yude Bai's Homepage

I am a lecturer at the School of Software, Tiangong University, holding a Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University. My research focuses on mobile security, including topics such as malware detection and malicious behavior reasoning. Feel free to contact me via email: baiyude@tiangong.edu.cn.
